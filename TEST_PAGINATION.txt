Pagination buttons should be visible at the bottom of the table.

If you don't see them:
1. Scroll down to the bottom of the employees table
2. Look for "Previous" and "Next" buttons
3. Check if you have more than 10 employees

The pagination shows:
- Page X of Y
- Previous button (disabled on page 1)
- Next button (disabled on last page)
- Showing 1-10 of 50 employees

If still not visible, refresh the page and check console for errors.
